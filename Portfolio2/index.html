I met my first computer when I was very small and computers often took up whole rooms. My dad was a computer engineer and on the weekends, his company that built computers was often my playground. Now computers fit on our laps (even on our wrists!) and my laptop has become my playground.

I love building websites that are smart, clean and user friendly. I build my sites from the ground up, using responsive design with a mobile first approach. I start with HTML, pretty it up with CSS, and add in Javascript and JQuery to make make my sites polished and interactive. Visit karenjnelson.com for more information.
